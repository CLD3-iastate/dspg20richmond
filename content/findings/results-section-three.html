Modeling Census Tract Level Evictions: Visualization of socio-demographic variables and eviction rates in Richmond 2013-2018

To provide further guidance for policymakers interested in understanding evictions in Richmond, we provide a dashboard that can be used to visualize the socio-demographic variables that our statistical model identified as being associated with eviction rates. This dashboard provides some context for what we can observe about the city of Richmond and the variation across census tracts and time.
