Discussion and Next Steps

We determined that state level landlord and tenant friendly policies are significantly associated with tract level eviction rates. These finding suggested that state level policy levers such as:

  • Laws that reduce protected class exemptions in housing-related transactions,
  • Laws that protect tenants from retaliation by landlords,
  • Laws that reduce the number of exemptions landlords have from state landlord-tenant law can affect evictions rates.
